Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

To book a ride, go to the Passenger Dashboard and click 'Book Now'.

How can I contact my driver?

Once your ride is confirmed, you can contact your driver through the app.

How do I update my payment method?

Go to the Payment Methods section in your dashboard and update your details.
